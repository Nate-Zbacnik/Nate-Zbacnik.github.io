Mersnik College Football Ratings 2018

  • I created a ratings system for college football teams based on points for/against. Each team is given two ratings: one for offense, and one for defense. By strategically sampling games from the entire season, a probability distribution function representing team skill can be created, representing the likelihood that a team plays at a given level. This allows for more nuance to be given when considering the strength of team, rather than attempting to blend games when they performed well and games when they performed poorly into a single rating.
